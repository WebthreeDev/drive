FAQ

WHAT IS ETH Drive?

ETH Drive is a Real Driving Metaverse. You will be able to purchase NFT of real cars, and build your ultimate and unique car collection. You can also trade, rent or drive your cars in multiple championships and competitions. No matter the type of player you are, there is always something fun to do in ETH Drive.

HOW IS ETH Drive FINANCED?

ETH Drive is developed by an experienced video games studio, The Tiny Digital Factory. The project is supported by investors, as well as with the profits earned from the other games of the company. The finances extracted from the NFT sales are immediately re-invested into ETH Drive, for example as an additional budget in order to bring more cars and new licenses in the game.

WHICH BLOCKCHAIN DOES ETH Drive USE?

ETH Drive is using the Solana Blockchain and the Polygon Blockchain.

WHY SUPPORT AN ADDITIONAL BLOCKCHAIN, SUCH AS POLYGON?

This decision has been thought about for a long time, there are multiple reasons behind this:

– Users security: We often advise users to use a ledger to protect the integrity of the assets and MetaMask is the first wallet to integrate Ledger. Using an offline wallet such as the Ledger Nano is the most efficient and secure way to store the private keys, which control the crypto-assets you own. The Ledger Nano stores your private keys offline, so that no one on the Internet can corrupt them. This is why the combination of Ledger and MetaMask is considered the most secure and harmonious alternative.


– Protecting ETH Drive: Being on multiple blockchains ensure us that the game won’t close because a blockchain went down. This way we are protecting users assets.


– Interoperability: As we wish to create an interoperability between games, it will be more easier for the realization of this project if we’re supporting the Polygon Blockchain as a lot of racing games are on it. Our goal is to create an ecosystem between games!

CAN I CONNECT MULTIPLE WALLETS TO A SAME ACCOUNT?

Yes, we will support multiple wallets: Phantom & MetaMask.

WHAT DOES THE ETH Drive GAMEPLAY LOOK LIKE?

ETH Drive is a simulation where driving skills are important. Each car has unique configurations. Practicing and learning will be important if you want to win BIG!

HOW CAN I PLAY THE GAME?

The Closed Alpha will be Early Access-based, planned for this summer. Players who participated in the Car Series NFTs First Mint will be able to join the adventure and have the very first look at ETH Drive’s early gameplay.
The Official Open Alpha will be available to all with free tryouts so everyone can enjoy the game. It’s set to be released Q4 2022.
While the experience will be limited for F2P Player, the game will however be fully F2P by 2023.

HOW CAN I BUY NFT CARS?

Please make sure to join our Telegram and Discord communities to be the first informed about the upcoming sales.

WHICH REWARDS CAN I WIN IN ETH Drive?

Depending on the event, different rewards will be distributed. Drivers can expect the following:

  • ETH Drive Tokens (IDT)
  • Customization Items
  • Car Upgrades
  • Limited Cars
  • Car XP and Driver Fame
WHAT ARE YOUR FUTURE DEVELOPMENT PLANS FOR ETH Drive?

Through time, the game will continuously improve and add new content. Everything will be decided with the community. What to add? What to change or to improve? The players and car owners will decide!
